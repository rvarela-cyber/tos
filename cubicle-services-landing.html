Cubicle Configuration, Upgrade & Repair

Optimize Your Workspace Layout

Your office layout needs to evolve with your business. For 40+ years, our furniture specialists have been reconfiguring, repairing, and upgrading cubicle systems for businesses across Dallas-Fort Worth, Austin and San Antonio.

Whether you need to add workstations for a growing team, reconfigure an entire floor for better collaboration, or repair damaged panels before a lease turnover, our technicians handle it all. We work with all major panel system manufacturers and maintain an extensive parts inventory.

Cubicle Service Options

Cubicle Reconfiguration

Reorganize your existing cubicle systems to accommodate new team structures, improve traffic flow, or maximize your floor space. We disassemble, relocate, and reassemble panels to match your new layout requirements.

[PHOTO PLACEHOLDER - PANEL REPAIR]

Panel Repair & Replacement

Damaged fabric, dented frames, broken connectors. Our technicians repair or replace individual panel components without disrupting your entire workstation system. We source parts from our extensive inventory.

[PHOTO PLACEHOLDER - WORK SURFACE]

Work Surface Modification

Resize, reshape, refinish, or replace work surfaces to meet your specific needs. Our technicians can cut surfaces to accommodate new equipment, refinish worn tops, or install entirely new work surfaces.

[PHOTO PLACEHOLDER - PANEL CLEANING]

Panel Cleaning

Remove years of accumulated dirt, dust, and stains from cubicle panel fabric. Our cleaning process refreshes your workspace appearance and extends the life of your panel systems. Service performed after hours.

[PHOTO PLACEHOLDER - ELECTROSTATIC]

Electrostatic Painting

Give metal panel frames and components a fresh, durable finish through electrostatic painting. This process provides a smooth, factory-quality coating at a fraction of replacement cost.

[PHOTO PLACEHOLDER - LOCK KEY]

Lock & Key Services

Add locks to existing storage pedestals, re-key cabinets for new employees, or upgrade lock systems for enhanced security. We offer keyed alike, keyed separately, or keyed by sequence options.

All Major Panel Systems

Our technicians are trained on current and legacy product lines from all major manufacturers. We work with Herman Miller, Steelcase, Haworth, Knoll, Teknion, Allsteel, and many others.

Our technicians are experienced with both current and legacy panel systems that other companies won't touch. Our parts inventory includes hardware and components from discontinued product lines.

Parts Sourcing

If a part is no longer available from the manufacturer, we often have it in our inventory or can salvage it from decommissioned systems. This allows us to extend the life of your existing investment.

Property Managers & Lease Turnovers

When tenants vacate, furniture often remains. We help property managers assess, repair, and reconfigure existing cubicle systems to make the space move-in ready for new tenants.

Our services include panel cleaning to remove years of wear, repairs to damaged components, and reconfiguration to match new tenant requirements. We provide detailed assessments and formal proposals before any work begins.
