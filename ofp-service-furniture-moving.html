What Office Furniture Moving Actually Means

Four Moves That Aren’t Office Relocations.

Most companies hear “office moving” and picture lots of boxes, files, and IT cables. Office moves, big and small, we handle that too through our sister company Move Solutions. But this page is about something different. We handle the office furniture-side work that happens before, during, and after a project. The pieces, not the people.

You’re refreshing, downsizing, or starting over. Existing furniture needs to leave.

We handle the assessment, valuation, and physical removal. Pieces with resale value can be credited toward your project. Pieces without value get responsibly disposed of, recycled, or donated. Either way, your space ends up empty and your conscience stays clean.

Frequently Asked Questions

Office Furniture Moving Questions, Answered.

The questions that come up when office furniture has to leave, arrive, shift, or wait.

Do you move office furniture I bought from another dealer?
Yes. We move office furniture we sold and office furniture customers bought elsewhere. We are familiar with every major manufacturer line, so handling, disassembly, and reassembly are not a problem regardless of where the office furniture came from.
Can you hold my office furniture while my new space is being built out?
Yes. We have 400,000 square feet of climate-controlled warehouse space. Your office furniture is inventoried, photographed, and held until your space is ready. We coordinate redelivery and installation when you say go.
How long can you warehouse my office furniture?
Short-term storage is included for active projects between phases. For extended storage beyond the project timeline, your account manager will provide rates based on volume and duration. Most clients use storage for weeks, not months.
What happens to office furniture I do not want anymore?
We assess every piece for resale value. Office furniture in good condition gets purchased back from you, often credited against your new furniture or labor costs. Pieces without resale value get donated, recycled, or responsibly disposed of. Documentation provided for your sustainability reporting.
Can you move office furniture within my existing building?
Yes. Internal moves, restacks, floor reorganizations, department swaps. We disassemble, protect your building, transport through the space, and reassemble to the new layout. Common for clients reorganizing without relocating.
Will you coordinate with my office mover?
Yes. When you have a separate mover handling people, files, and IT, we coordinate the office furniture-side work with their schedule. The two trades stay out of each other’s way and your overall move stays on time.
What if my office furniture is older or no longer manufactured?
We work with current and legacy product lines that many dealers will not touch. Our parts inventory includes hardware and components from discontinued systems. If something cannot be reassembled, we tell you up front rather than mid-project.
Do you move office furniture between cities or states?
Yes. Within Texas we use our own trucks and teams. For cross-state and nationwide office furniture moves, we work through the Office Moving Alliance, a vetted network with consistent standards. One OFP project manager owns the engagement either way.
