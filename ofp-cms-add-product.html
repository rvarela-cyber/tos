What this screen does (Screen 3). This is the standard WooCommerce Add New Product page, with the Product Data box pre-set to Consignment and the consignor (Sarah Reynolds / Lakewood Capital Partners) already linked, shown in the purple banner at the top. The Consignment tab in the Product Data box is auto-selected and exposes the per-product attributes (Pricing, Availability & Lifecycle, Internal Notes) the developer must add. Everything else on the page — title, description, image gallery, categories, brands, publish box — is the unmodified WooCommerce screen the team already uses. Clicking Publish in the right sidebar pushes the product live (or schedules it for the Auto-Publish Date).
