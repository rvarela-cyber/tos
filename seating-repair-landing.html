Office Seating Repair & Cleaning

Expert Chair Care for Your Office Environment

For 40+ years, our furniture specialists have been repairing and restoring office seating for businesses across Dallas-Fort Worth, Austin and San Antonio. From broken cylinders and torn upholstery to coffee-stained fabric, our technicians have the expertise to get your office chairs working like new again. We service all major manufacturers and handle jobs of any size.

Before we touch a single office chair, we inspect it. We photograph the manufacturer information, check the serial number, and verify warranty status. Many office chairs remain under warranty, and our team will confirm coverage before recommending repairs. If parts are available, we source them. If parts are no longer available, we recommend replacement and can connect you with our sister company Office Furniture Plus for new replacement options.

From a single executive office chair to an entire building, no job is too small or too large.

Six Reasons Business Owners Call Us First

Warranty Check First

We inspect manufacturer info and serial numbers before quoting. If your office chairs remain under warranty, our team will confirm coverage, potentially saving you the repair cost entirely.

After-Hours Service

Cleaning and repairs happen when people aren't at their desks, minimizing disruption to operations. We work around your schedule, including evenings and weekends.

40+ Years Experience

Formal Proposals

On-Site or Drop-Off

What We Fix

  • Pneumatic cylinder replacement (sinking office chairs)
  • Mechanism repair (tilt, recline, height adjustment)
  • Arm replacement and repair
  • Seat pad replacement (when parts available)
  • Upholstery repair and reupholstery
  • Caster and base replacement

Service Options

Most repairs are completed on-site at your location during a scheduled visit. For complex repairs requiring specialized equipment, office chairs can be brought to our facility and returned within two business days.

Parts Inventory

Our on-site shop maintains an extensive inventory of salvaged hardware accumulated over decades of service, allowing us to source hard-to-find parts for older office chair models.

Brands We Service

We service all major office furniture manufacturers including Herman Miller, Steelcase, Haworth, Knoll, and HON. Our technicians are trained on current and legacy product lines.

Office Seating Cleaning

[PHOTO PLACEHOLDER - OFFICE SEATING CLEANING]

Our Cleaning Process

  • Pre-treat for specific stain types (protein, oil, ink)
  • Targeted scrubbing of affected areas
  • Hot water extraction for deep cleaning
  • Professional-grade stain removal
  • Approximately 4 hours dry time
  • After-hours scheduling available

Honest Expectations

Our process removes the majority of stains, including coffee, food, dirt, and shoe marks. Some stains are permanent, and our team will advise you upfront if we identify any that cannot be removed.

When Cleaning Matters

Preparing a space for new tenants or a company visit requires furniture that looks presentable. Our professional cleaning restores your seating to like-new condition, often overnight.

Pricing

Office chair cleaning is typically calculated per-chair with a minimum of 50 chairs for on-site service. For smaller jobs, we provide custom time-and-material estimates upon request.
