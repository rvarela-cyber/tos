Our Process

From First Call To Installation Day.

A made-to-order project runs 16 weeks from start to finish. Here's what happens in each stage, what we need from you, and what you receive at the end of each step.

WeeksStepWhat HappensWhat You Receive
Week 1 Discovery & Programming Your account manager asks the essential questions about timeline, budget, headcount, and the kind of work that happens in your space. The deeper this conversation, the better the result. Clarity on which furniture path (new, pre-owned, import, or blend) fits your situation.
Weeks 2-3 Space Planning You share CAD drawings (or we request them from your property manager). Our design team creates a preliminary layout: workstation and office counts, traffic flow, functional optimization. Professional 2D space plan with verified workstation count and preliminary design direction.
Weeks 3-5 Design Presentation & Budget Review We present 2-3 options at different price points with visual renderings of your future space. This is where the honest budget conversation happens. You tell us what feels right. Design presentation, budgetary pricing for your preferred option, and renderings of your future space.
Weeks 5-7 Detailed Specification & Approval For made-to-order pieces, you select specific finishes: wood tones, laminates, fabrics, hardware. We itemize every part. Project manager quotes installation labor. Complete formal proposal, detailed specification document, and final pricing.
Weeks 8-15 Order & Manufacturing You sign and submit the 50% deposit. We submit orders immediately. Manufacturing runs 4-8 weeks. Meanwhile, we coordinate with your GC, electrician, and IT vendor. Order acknowledgments from each manufacturer, estimated delivery dates, and a locked-in installation schedule.
Week 16 Installation Furniture arrives, gets inspected at our warehouse, and our uniformed installation team delivers and installs per the approved plan. We connect to power/data, clean up, and walk through with you. Fully installed, ready-to-use furniture with warranty documentation and a final walk-through to make sure every piece is exactly where it should be.

Frequently Asked Questions

Space Planning Questions, Answered.

Honest answers to the questions we hear most about space planning, design, and how a project actually comes together.

Do you charge for space planning?
A complimentary space planning consultation is included with your furniture purchase. We create a professional 2D space plan, design presentation, and budgetary direction as part of the consultation. There's no upfront cost and no obligation to move forward.
Can you work with my interior designer?
Absolutely. Interior designers bring us in on projects all the time, and we love it. The designer leads the aesthetic and client relationship. We handle furniture specification, manufacturer coordination, ordering, freight, and installation. Bring your designer to the first meeting.
What do I need to bring to a space planning consultation?
CAD drawings of your space (or the property manager's contact info so we can request them), your target headcount including any anticipated growth, a rough budget range, and a target operational date. If you don't have all of this, that's fine. The consultation helps you figure out what you don't know yet.
What if I don't have CAD drawings of my space?
No problem. Our team can request them from your property manager or building owner. If drawings don't exist, our design team can field-measure your space. We also have software that converts multiple file formats to CAD, so even a PDF or older drawing usually works.
How long does the space planning process take?
A preliminary 2D space plan typically takes 1 to 2 weeks after the discovery meeting. Design presentation and rendering follow in another 2 to 3 weeks. Total time from first call to approved design varies based on project complexity and how quickly decisions get made on your end.
Will I see what my space will look like before I buy?
Yes. You'll receive a 2D space plan showing the layout and 3D renderings showing finishes, furniture, and atmosphere. You can see and approve every element before a single piece is ordered. Most clients receive 2 to 3 design options at different price points to choose from.
What happens if my headcount or layout changes mid-project?
Talk to us as soon as it happens. The earlier a change comes up, the more we can do about it. If furniture hasn't been ordered yet, we adjust the plan. If it's already in manufacturing, options narrow but we still have flexibility on quantities, install sequence, and warehousing.
Can you space-plan a workspace anywhere in the country?
Yes. While our showrooms are in Texas (Irving, Austin, San Antonio), we plan and furnish workspaces nationwide. Discovery happens by phone or video, you email us CAD drawings, we share plans and renderings digitally, and we coordinate installation through our nationwide partner network.
